Problem Statement

Acme Corp operates 12 branches across 4 countries in Latin America. Each branch runs a local SAP R/3 instance for accounting, generating financial data in different currencies and chart of accounts. The consolidation process is entirely manual: analysts export data via spreadsheets, apply FX conversion and intercompany elimination rules manually, and consolidate into a master spreadsheet. The process is slow, error-prone, and lacks an audit trail.

  • Financial Analysts (4 people) — spend 12h/week each on low-value manual work
  • Controller — cannot close before D+8, impacting C-level decision-making
  • CFO — had to restate board reports 3 times due to consolidation errors
  • Auditors — no audit trail; every review requires manual recalculation

REG-PROD-04 Value Proposition Highlight Card + Elevator Pitch

For controllership teams at multi-branch companies who need to consolidate financial data from different ERPs quickly and reliably, FinTrack Pro is an automated financial consolidation platform that centralizes data, applies intercompany elimination rules, and generates real-time consolidated reports. Unlike Oracle HFM or manual spreadsheets, our product is no-code configurable, costs 40% less, and natively integrates with SAP, TOTVS, and Oracle.

REG-PROD-09 Product Vision Quote-Style Card

FinTrack Pro transforms financial consolidation from a manual, slow, and error-prone process into an automated, auditable, and real-time operation. Controllership teams stop being spreadsheet operators and become strategic business analysts.

Where we will be in 3 years (2029)

In 2029, FinTrack Pro will be the reference platform for financial consolidation of mid-market companies in Latin America. Over 200 companies will use the platform to consolidate data from thousands of branches in real time, with automatic compliance for IFRS, CPC, and local regulations.

The platform will have evolved from a consolidation tool into a financial intelligence hub:

Close prediction (estimate results before official close)
ML-powered anomaly detection for atypical entries
Anonymous benchmarking across same-segment clients
Marketplace of connectors for regional ERPs

Product Principles

  • 1The controller is the hero — every feature must empower the controller, never complicate their work
  • 2Reliable data or nothing — we'd rather delay a feature than deliver non-traceable data
  • 3Complexity in the engine, simplicity on screen — complex IFRS rules abstracted into simple configurations
  • 4Latin America first — prioritize LatAm regulations, currencies, and ERPs before global expansion
  • 5Open by default — open APIs, flexible exports, zero data lock-in
